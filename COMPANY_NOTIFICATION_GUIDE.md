# Company Notification Experience - Step by Step Guide 📱

## What Happens When Department Head Assigns a Student

### **Timeline of Events**

```
Time: 10:00 AM - Department Head assigns John Doe to Tech Corp
         ↓
Time: 10:00 AM - System creates ACCEPTED application
         ↓
Time: 10:00 AM - Notification sent to Student (John Doe)
         ↓
Time: 10:00 AM - Notification sent to Company (Tech Corp) ✅
         ↓
Time: 10:00 AM - Company sees notification in real-time
```

## 💼 Step 6: Company Takes Action

### **Available Actions for Company:**

1. **Contact Student**
   - Send email directly from platform
   - Call using provided phone number
   - Schedule onboarding meeting

2. **Prepare Onboarding**
   - Create onboarding checklist
   - Prepare workspace and equipment
   - Assign mentor/supervisor
   - Schedule orientation

3. **Review Student Profile**
   - Check skills and experience
   - Review previous projects
   - Assess fit for position
   - Plan training program

4. **Coordinate Start Date**
   - Confirm internship start date
   - Arrange first day logistics
   - Send welcome package
   - Provide company information
