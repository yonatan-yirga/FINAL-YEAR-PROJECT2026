## 🎯 Usage

Navigate to: `http://localhost:5173/department/advisor-overload`

### Workflow
1. **View Statistics** - See overloaded and available advisors at a glance
2. **Select Overloaded Advisor** - Click on a red card to view their students
3. **Select Students** - Choose students to reassign (checkbox selection)
4. **Choose Target Advisor** - Select from the table of available advisors
5. **Confirm Reassignment** - Click the action bar button to complete
